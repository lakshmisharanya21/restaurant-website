HISTORY

Three engineering best friends, each with a shared passion for food, decided to leave behind their nine-to-five careers to open a restaurant that combines their love for culinary creativity with a commitment to innovation. Together, they bring a mix of technical precision, problem-solving skills, and a deep appreciation for diverse flavors to the table. From designing an efficient kitchen layout to implementing sustainable food practices, their engineering backgrounds allow them to optimize every detail. Driven by a dream of crafting a unique dining experience, they aim to create a welcoming space where every dish tells a story, blending the precision of their engineering minds with the artistry of cooking.
